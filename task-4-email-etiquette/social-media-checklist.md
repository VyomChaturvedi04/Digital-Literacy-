**SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST**

# DO's

- Audit your privacy settings regularly to ensure only intended audiences see your personal posts.
- Think before you "Share" or "Retweet". Verify the source of information to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Network professionally by connecting with alumni and professors on platforms like LinkedIn.
- Use "The Billboard Test": Only post things you would be comfortable seeing on a giant billboard in your hometown.
- Give credit where it’s due. Always tag original creators when sharing art, photography, or ideas.

# DONT'S

- Don't post illegal or policy-violating content, such as underage drinking or academic dishonesty.
- Don't engage with "trolls" or online bullies. De-escalate by ignoring or blocking rather than arguing.
- Don't vent about employers or professors. Digital footprints are permanent and searchable by recruiters.
- Don't share sensitive personal data, such as your home address, current GPS location, or full birthdate.
- Don't post in the heat of the moment. Emotional "rage-posting" often leads to long-term reputational damage.
